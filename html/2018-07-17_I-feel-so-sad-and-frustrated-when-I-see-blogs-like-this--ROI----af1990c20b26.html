I feel so sad and frustrated when I see blogs like this; ROI ?? what has ROI to do with how long a painter tries to perfect his art or a poet the play of words, or companies like Toyota to strive endlessly for better quality. I have been coding for 18 years now and TDD is no mantra or cliche or buzzword that I blindly followed. At that time it was not even popular. I remember searching for the term and coming with Xtreme Programming. One should try doing complex C++ programming without TDD; I know what is it to be woken up at unholy hours due to SW bugs; that I started doing TDD to keep my sanity. My heart used to jump at one time when I heard the telephone ring, because I worked in a small company initially whose clients were on the other side of the globe and they had no qualms about calling even in the middle of the night; hell they even send a developer knocking at my house when I did not answer,.. nightmares, somehow relate well to the Coding Horror blogs. Test-driven development kept my bug count way way down. It is not that GCed languages are better; one should try writing a few layers of the component in a dynamically typed lang like Python without TDD and you will start pulling your hair out. TDD is a ROI for your mental peace. Maybe some organizations are so fat that devs are shielded heavily or some developers do not care; for me, quality is the only thing that a blue-collar programmer like me worship; and for that God, I don’t mind to invest my time irrespective of the return I get or not..
