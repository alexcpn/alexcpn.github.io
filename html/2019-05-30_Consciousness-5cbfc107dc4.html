Consciousness

A Gold Fish in a Bowl

Your consciousness is like a Goldfish,
Swimming round and round and round,
Alone in a bowl.
A billion minds, a billion bowls
Staring at each other,
Seeing each other go,
Swimming round and round,
In the confines of the limited mind.

By Alex Punnen on May 30, 2019.
