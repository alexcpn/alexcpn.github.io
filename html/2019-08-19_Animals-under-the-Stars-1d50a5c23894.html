Animals under the Stars

What do we Dream when there is nothing to dream

What is it we dream of,
When we have nothing to fear of,
And nothing to want ?

Money a mere number,
That buys things which we no longer cherish,
A way to pass time ,
A way not to have time to dream,
As we have no dreams.

What is it we dream of ?
Are memories taking us far back,
When we had no memories yet.

The age of innocence,
When everything was full of hope.
We can’t bear the now,
The idle time that needs to be filled,
As we are afraid to close our eyes,
As we have no more dreams to dream.

What is it we dream of,
When we find that everything is the same,
Love hate pleasure and pain,
A way of passing time,
As we pass through time.

We can dream of nothing that is ours,
As all are but memories gathering dust,
Or things that yet stand ,
But rots away or taken away with time.

We can dream of nothing ,
As we will have nothing,
But what is gone or given away.

What is it we can dream of,
We the animals under the stars.

I dare not dream, I dare not close,
As I fear to confront,
The darkness that is in store.

How lucky are the ones,
Who in that darkness are blessed,
With a light of hope,
A smile lit once long ago,
On the face of another.

What is it we dream of,
When there is nothing more to dream?
Only the smiles in faces,
That we chanced to light,
As we pass through time.

By Alex Punnen on August 19, 2019.
