I spread my arms wide,

To hug away you hate.

I spread my arms wide,

To hug away you hate.

I will eat what you eat, or not,

The things you hate or hold sacred,

I will learn your tongue, and speak like you,

Believe in your God, as yours is as good as mine,

I will laugh with you, and share your pains,

In the light maybe I look different, or maybe not.

In the dark you will think I am one of you.

Can you still hug me under the sun ?

It’s not the clan, it’s not the skin, you say,

Can you hate what we hate?

And then you are truly one.

Your hate is a leporous bond,

To lose my humanity,is to lose my life,

It is too steep a cost,for that bond.

Let me spread my arms wide,

To hug away your hate.

That you can be born again,

Truly baptized in humanity.

By Alex Punnen on December 18, 2018.
